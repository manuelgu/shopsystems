## Functionality

Spryker is suitable for B2C [üåê](https://spryker.com/app/uploads/2021/03/2021_Handout_Spryker_B2C_Features_EN.pdf)  B2B [üåê](https://spryker.com/app/uploads/2021/03/2021_Handout_Spryker_B2B_Features_EN.pdf) and Marketplace use cases. It includes all standard features along with additional, complex functionality that most of its competitors do not offer, like measurement/packing units, gift cards, punch out, approvals, quotation, etc.

Spryker can be combined with other solutions, but is also capable to run in a stand-alone mode, because it‚Äôs fully equipped with most required features.
