# About us (ROQ)

<div className="highlightText">ROQ manages complex e-commerce projects end-to-end by leveraging and empowering in-house
    IT teams.</div>

ROQ offers an alternative approach to outsourcing the project!

ROQ is a consultancy boutique that enables companies to execute complex e-commerce projects in-house, without relying entirely on external agencies.

* With an agency, you (hopefully) get a new shop
* With ROQ, you get a modern e-commerce system and a highly capable IT organization equipped to manage it for the long term
