## Functionality

### Feature overview

Vendure is ideal for B2C use cases and offers flexibility for basic marketplace scenarios thanks to its ‚Äúmulti-everything‚Äù approach.

While not specifically designed for B2B, Vendure is still commonly used in these scenarios due to its simple architecture and ease of development.

Vendure also supports enterprise-level features, such as SSO integration with providers like Okta.

Although Vendure is open-source, some official features require separate licensing. For example, the ‚Äúwishlist‚Äù feature costs ‚Ç¨480 per year.
[üåê](https://vendure.io/hub/vendure-wishlist-plugin)
